5 Tips to Stay Safe in Your First Gig

Written by
Rose Hernandez

Your first step into the adult entertainment industry can be exciting, but safety should always come first. Here are 5 essential tips to help you protect yourself, stay confident, and feel empowered during your first professional experiences.

1. Research Before You Agree

Always research the agencies, creators, and companies you plan to work with. Look for reviews, professional websites, and signs of a transparent, respectful process.

2. Set Clear Boundaries

Before agreeing to any project, clearly communicate what you are and aren’t comfortable with. Your limits should be respected at all times.

3. Bring Support When Possible

Whenever allowed, bring a trusted friend, manager, or advocate to your first meetings or shoots. Having a witness adds an extra layer of protection.

4. Trust Your Instincts

If anything feels off — even slightly — trust yourself. You are never obligated to stay in an uncomfortable situation, no matter the pressure.

5. Understand Your Rights

You have legal rights to a safe and consensual working environment. If something violates your comfort, you can stop and seek help without penalty.

"Protecting yourself is not just smart — it’s empowering. You deserve to feel confident, respected, and safe at every step."
