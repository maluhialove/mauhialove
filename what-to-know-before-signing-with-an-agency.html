What to Know Before Signing With an Agency

Written by
Rose Hernandez

Choosing to work with an agency is a big decision that can shape your career in the adult entertainment industry. Here’s what you should know — and what you deserve — before ever putting pen to paper.

1. Know What Services They Offer

A great agency does more than just book you gigs. They advocate for you, help manage contracts, and support your long-term goals. Make sure their services match your needs.

2. Understand the Fine Print

Never rush into signing. Read every contract carefully, and make sure you understand exclusivity clauses, commissions, and cancellation policies before committing.

3. Transparency Is Key

Good agencies are upfront about their fees, expectations, and processes. If anything feels hidden, confusing, or rushed — that's a red flag.

4. You Always Have the Final Say

No agency should pressure you into jobs you aren’t comfortable with. Remember: you have control over your career and your choices.

5. Listen to Your Gut

Beyond contracts and offers, trust your instincts. If an agency feels aligned with your values and goals, that’s a partnership worth considering.

"An agency should amplify your voice — not replace it. You deserve representation that respects your worth."
